SMOTE

Synthetic Minority Over-sampling Technique

Regular SMOTE

$s_i=x_i+(x_{zi}-x_i)\times\lambda,\ \lambda \in [0,1]$


Borderline SMOTE

$s_i=x_i+(p_i^{'}-x_i)\times\lambda,\ \lambda \in [0,1]$


SVM SMOTE

$s_i=sv_i+(sv_i-x_{im})\times\lambda,\ \lambda \in [0,1]$


$s_i=sv_i+(x_{im}-sv_i)\times\lambda,\ \lambda \in [0,1]$


ADASYN

Adaptive Synthetic Sampling Approach for Imbalanced Learning



  • same as SMOTE just with a minor improvement
  • adds a random small values to the points thus making it have a little more variance
  • uilizes the density distribution of a minority sample as criteria for how much synthetic data should be created.
  • helps focus attention on minority examples depending on how difficult they are to learn

More detail about ADASYN

$r_i=\frac{\Delta_i}K,\ i=1,2,...m_s$

$\hat{r_i}=r_i/\sum_{i=1}^{m_s}r_i$

$g_i=\hat{r_i}\times G$

  • $\Delta_i$: $x_i$的KNN中少数类的数量
  • $m_s, \ m_l$: 分别代表少数类和多数类的数量
  • 不平衡度 $d=m_s/m_l$
  • 少数类需要生成的样本数为： $G=(m_l-m_s)\times \beta$

Summary

  • Naïve Bayes was best paired with ADASYN.
  • Logistic regression and SVM were best paired with borderline-SMOTE2.
